Biography

Yi Song is currently a PhD candidate in School of Computer Science, Wuhan University, China, under the supervision of professor Xiaoyuan Xie. He is also a member of CSTAR - Centre of Software Testing, Analysis, and Reliability (2020 ~ ). His research interests mainly include software testing, debugging, program analysis, and search-based software engineering. Any cooperation is warmly welcomed, and more information can be found at GitHub and Google Scholar.

Before that, he received B.Sc. and M.Phil. degrees in Computer Science from China Three Gorges University in 2018 and 2020, respectively, and received his dual bachelor's degree from Communication University of China in 2018. He visited Advanced Research Center on Software Testing and Quality Assurance at The University of Texas at Dallas as a short-term scholar from April to June 2019.
